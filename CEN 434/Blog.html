The Basics of Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity protects systems and networks from attacks or unauthorized access. It includes tools and practices like firewalls, antivirus software, encryption, and ethical hacking. Learning cybersecurity is essential to safeguard information and maintain trust in the digital world.
